# Privacy Policy for Sportinkaart

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

## Introduction

Sportinkaart ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly

When you use certain features of Sportinkaart, we may collect:

- **Contact Information**: Name and email address (only when you submit suggestions, questions, or request changes to location information)
- **User-Generated Content**: Any information you provide when suggesting new sports locations or requesting updates to existing locations

### Information stored locally on your device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Favorites**: Sports clubs you've saved as favorites
- **Language Preference**: Your selected app language (Dutch or English)
- **Search History**: Recent searches within the app

### Information automatically collected

- **Sports Location Data**: We retrieve information about sports clubs and facilities from our database to display in the app
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To respond to your requests**: Contact information submitted through suggestion forms is used solely to respond to your questions or process your location suggestions
- **To improve our service**: Understanding which regions and sports are most popular helps us expand our coverage
- **To provide core functionality**: Displaying sports club information, filtering, and search features

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage**: Sports club information is stored in our Supabase database
- **Local Storage**: Your favorites and preferences are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage)
- **Email Communications**: When you submit a suggestion or question, your email and information are transmitted securely and stored only as long as necessary to respond to your request

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase**: Database hosting for sports club information
- **Email Service**: To send and receive suggestion form submissions

These services may have access to your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time, which will prevent any future data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: **info@sportinkaart.nl**

## Children's Privacy

Sportinkaart is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- **Suggestion Form Data**: Retained only as long as necessary to respond to your request, typically no more than 90 days
- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear the data manually

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying a notice in the app (for material changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using Sportinkaart, you consent to such transfers.
